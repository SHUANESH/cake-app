Chocolate Cake

Ingredients for chocolate cake

  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 2 cups white sugar
  3. ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa
  4. 2 teaspoons baking soda
  5. 1 teaspoon baking powder
  6. ½ teaspoon sal
  7. 2 eggs
  8. 1 cup cold brewed coffee
  9. 1 cup milk
  10. ½ cup vegetable oil
  11. 2 teaspoons vinegar

How to prepare:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar,
    cocoa, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Make
    a well in the center and pour in the eggs, coffee, milk,
    oil and vinegar. Mix until smooth; the batter will be thin. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
    3. Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes,
  3. or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
